    1. Accept CLI args: --model (path to .zip, default: python/models/ppo_run_1_interrupted.zip) and --vecnorm (path to .pkl, default: python/models/ppo_run_1_interrupted_vecnormalize.pkl)

    4. Wrap policy in OnnxableSB3Policy(th.nn.Module) that calls policy(obs, deterministic=True)
